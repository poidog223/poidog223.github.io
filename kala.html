Kala Alexander

Kala Alexander is the owner and operator of Go Fishing Hawaii. Born and raised on the island of Kauai, the ocean has always been a integral part of his life.

He is recognized as a world-class waterman and has participated in the prestigious Quiksilver Big Wave Surfing Event in Honor of Eddie Aikau at Waimea Bay. In addition to being a world-class waterman, Kala has also had featured roles in tv shows and movies including staring in Blue Crush and Hawaii 5-0.

Come fish with Kala on the Nani Kai
